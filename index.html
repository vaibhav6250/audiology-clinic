Hearing Aid Guide

Types of Hearing Aids We Offer

We offer multiple styles of digital hearing aids to match different hearing needs, comfort levels, and cosmetic preferences. Our specialists will help you find the perfect fit.

Hearing Aid Styles Guide
From left to right: microRIE · RIE/RIC · BTE · IIC · CIC · ITC — all styles available at our clinic
🔋
Rechargeable

Rechargeable Models

Available in BTE and RIC styles — simply place on the charger overnight. No battery changes needed, ideal for all age groups.

👂
BTE

Behind the Ear

Sits behind the ear with a tube connecting to an ear mould. Suitable for all levels of hearing loss, very durable and easy to handle.

🎧
RIC / RITE

Receiver in Canal

Slim, discreet design with the receiver sitting inside the ear canal. Ideal for mild to moderate hearing loss with a natural sound quality.

🔵
ITE

In the Ear

Custom-made shell that fills the outer ear. Easy to insert and remove, popular choice for seniors and those with dexterity concerns.

⚪
CIC

Completely in Canal

Fits almost entirely inside the ear canal, making it nearly invisible. Great for mild to moderate hearing loss with cosmetic preferences.

🔘
IIC

Invisible in Canal

The most discreet style available — sits deep in the canal and is completely invisible when worn. Premium choice for active lifestyles.

Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How does hearing testing work?
+
We do a comprehensive and detailed testing — firstly pure tone audiometry to find your hearing threshold, and secondly speech audiometry to find the level at which you can identify and understand speech, because speech is what we use to communicate rather than tones.
How long does a hearing aid fitting take?
+
A typical fitting takes 45 minutes to 1 hour. We programme the device to your audiogram, explain how to use and care for it, and answer all your questions. Follow-up fine-tuning appointments are included in our care package.
What hearing aid brands do you offer?
+
We are authorised dealers for Widex, ReSound, and Starkey — covering the full range from affordable digital models to premium rechargeable and invisible-in-canal devices. We also carry several other leading international brands to suit every budget.
Do you provide speech therapy for adults?
+
Absolutely. We offer speech-language therapy for adults with stuttering, voice disorders, aphasia (post-stroke), dysarthria, and other communication challenges. Our therapists design individualized rehabilitation plans for every patient.
What areas of Patna do you serve?
+
Our clinic is located near Dinkar Golambar on Arya Kumar Road, Patna. We serve patients from Machhua Toli, Kankarbagh, Boring Road, Rajendra Nagar, and all across Patna and Bihar. Home visit consultations are available for elderly or mobility-challenged patients.
