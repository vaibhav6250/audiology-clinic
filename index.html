Frequently Asked Questions

How does hearing testing works?
+
We do a comprehensive and detailed testing firstly pure tone audiometry to find your hearing threshold and secondly speech audiometry as well to find about the level at which you can identify and understand speech, because speech is what we use to communicate rather than tones.
How long does a hearing aid fitting take?
+
A typical fitting takes 45 minutes to 1 hour. We programme the device to your audiogram, explain how to use and care for it, and answer all your questions. Follow-up fine-tuning appointments are included in our care package.
What hearing aid brands do you offer?
+
We are authorised dealers for Widex, ReSound, and Starkey — covering the full range from affordable digital models to premium rechargeable and invisible-in-canal devices.
Do you provide speech therapy for adults?
+
Absolutely. We offer speech-language therapy for adults with stuttering, voice disorders, aphasia (post-stroke), dysarthria, and other communication challenges. Our therapists design individualized rehabilitation plans for every patient.
What areas of Patna do you serve?
+
Our clinic is located near Dinkar Golambar on Arya Kumar Road, Patna. We serve patients from Machhua Toli, Kankarbagh, Boring Road, Rajendra Nagar, and all across Patna and Bihar. Home visit consultations are available for elderly or mobility-challenged patients.
